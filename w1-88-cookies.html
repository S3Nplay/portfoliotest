2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your browser or device by websites, apps, online media, and advertisements. They enable the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.
