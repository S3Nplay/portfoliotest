EXP0 is the leading organizer of game festivals in Swansea and other regions. Founded 2016, the company specializes in creating immersive gaming events where gamers and industry professionals gather to interact, learn about new games, and network.

Thanks to the successful festivals held by the company, it has considerable experience in creating events that meet the needs of the gaming community. The main location for game festivals is Swansea Arena, which provides a comfortable and exciting environment for all participants.

Where will Game Fest take place?

Game Fest will be held at Swansea Arena, you can find more information about it here Location.

What is the date of Game Fest this year?

Game Fest will take place from August 9 to 11, 2024.

How can I buy tickets to the game festival?

Tickets can be purchased online on our website Tickets.

Are there discounts for group tickets?

Yes, we provide discounts for group tickets. Details can be found on the website Tickets.

What games will be featured at Game Fest?

Game Fest includes a wide selection of games, from classics to new releases. A detailed list is available on our website Exhibitors.

Can I bring my own consoles and games to the game festival?

Yes, you can bring your own consoles and games. We also provide some equipment on site.

What events and entertainment are available to visitors outside of the games?

Beyond games, our game festival includes Presentations to satisfy all tastes.

Will food and drinks be available at the festival?

Yes, on the territory of the festival there will be a variety of food and drinks for the enjoyment of guests.

For more information, you can contact us here Contact Us.
